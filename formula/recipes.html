How to cook everything...badly

My father always said a cook is only as good as their ingredients. After years of enduring his meals, I’ve come to understand what makes a dish truly exceptional. The masterpieces I create cater to an extremely refined palate if they don’t suit yours, it’s best you leave them to someone who truly appreciates the finer things

  1. Cheerio Dogs

    2. Ingredients

    • 2 Gourmet Sausages
    • 1 slice of American Cheese
    • Cheerios
    • Fat Free Milk

    Instructions

    1. Microwave 2 sausages for 20 seconds
    2. Fill a bowl with cheerios and milk
    3. Gentley place the two sausages ontop of the cheerios
    4. Add the slice of american cheese

  2. Sausage spaghetti

    3. Ingredients

    • 6 pack of sausages
    • Pack of spaghetti

    Instructions

    1. Slice each sausage into 3 pieces
    2. Stick spaghetti sticks into sausage
    3. Boil spaghetti sausage on stove for 6 mins on high heat
